About

Leeds.dart is a user group that meets in Leeds on the first Wednesday of every other month. We aim to create a community of Dart developers in Yorkshire and provide knowledgeable and informative talks around Dart.

What makes Dart so great?

Dart offers a super tight development stack, pure Dart version of Angular2, Flutter for cross-platform mobile development and so much more. Come join us and find out for yourself! If you're looking for more information about the Dart language then head over to dartlang.
